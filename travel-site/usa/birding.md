# Birding in Central Park 
February 8, 2026

Somewhere in Central Park's Ramble, you might hear a lot of birds chirping, and the ground full of seed shells. On the trees next to the walking path, you will see about ten bird feeders hung up on the trees. They are feeders set up by the [Linnaean Society of New York](https://www.instagram.com/p/DQUZqNME6GC/) to supply migrant birds. I discovered this spot earlier this week, and started a new hobby(?) of birding ever since. Or maybe I am just too bored and started to hang out alone in parks and take photos of birds despite the extreme cold weather in the city.

Long story short, I just want to share the birds I have spotted this past week!!
