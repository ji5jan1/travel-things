## Flight #1: EVA BR108 KHH-NRT
**August 9, 2025 (Sat)**
- 🕰️ 06:54 - 11:42 (3h48m)
- ↔️ 2429km
- 💺 Seat 33K (window)
- 🛩️ A321 (B-16213)
- 📺 No PTV 👎🏻

**Comments:** Weekend mornings in KHH is super busy! The queue to security check was looooong, but it went forward quite quickly. I really enjoy the Taiwanese hospitality on EVA Air's flights, and I look forward to experiencing it again the next time I visit Taiwan!
