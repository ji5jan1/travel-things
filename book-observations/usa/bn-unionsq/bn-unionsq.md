# Barnes & Noble Union Square 
📍 33 E 17th St, New York, NY 10003

What else can you say about this store? It's no doubt the best bookstore in NYC!

Though there're a lot of great indie bookstoresm like the Strand, you have to admit that B&N has the biggest storefront. They sell everything, from children's books to CDs, and even a space for events. I had luckily participated a talk for NYU Publishing students in November, 2024.
