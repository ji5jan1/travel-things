# Tainan Municipal Library

📍 No.3, Gongyuan N. Rd., North Dist., Tainan City 704033, Taiwan (R.O.C.)<br>
inside Tainan Park, next ot Hsu Shih Music Library

### 👍🏻
- Big building with a wide genres of books
- A lot of Taiwanese literature/ history books!!
- Designated area for multimedia
- Clear theme for each floor
- Staircase mini book exhibition


### 👎🏻
- Books are quite old and not wrapped, I can see   mold/ rust(?) stain on the edges
- Some books are not so updated? (like a 2012 SQL book)
- A lot of homeless and purposeless uncles wandering around (who are obviously not here to read), making the library kind of smelly...
