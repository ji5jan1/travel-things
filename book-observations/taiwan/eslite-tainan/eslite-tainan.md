# eslite spectrum Tainan
Kelly Leung | Tainan, Taiwan | June 2025  
📍 No. 76, Sec. 1, Zhonghua W. Rd., South Dist., Tainan City 702 [(Google Maps)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/DjuJ7gLxmpbpaGUG9)

- Soft opening on May 28, 2025
- Grand opening on June 14, 2025
- 4 floors, only 3-4/F are for bookstore
- Largest eslite store in Taiwan?
- Positioning: department store?

## Overall Review
- Whole thing is quite cramped, a lot of people and small isles
- Weak connectivity inside the building
- Store loocation is not convenient (away from city centre)
- Weak cellular connection (why???)

## Bookstore
There's not a lot of walking spaces around the store, so I recommend going on weekdays. The store building is actually big, but the interior design is just... not good enough.<br>
